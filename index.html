The St. Louis MetroMarket is a non-profit mobile farmer’s market that will serve all St. Louis area food deserts – communities that lack grocery stores – by providing direct access to fresh, healthy, affordable produce and advocating on issues related to food justice, hunger, and health.

We launch in the Summer 2015 at the JeffVanderLou community.

Access

We will transform our donated Metro Transit bus by removing the seats and railings and, in their place, installing custom shelving and refrigeration stocked with healthy foods grown in and around the city.

Education

We will pair access with education through our partnership with SLU’s Nutrition and Dietetics department and will provide nutrition education and weekly cooking demos, at the curbside.

Affordability

We will incentivize healthy eating by keeping our food affordable and to do this we engage local corporations that are interested in making the St. Louis MetroMarket available to their employees. Through value-driven exchange, St. Louis corporations and their employees serve as both primary donors and investors in our social enterprise.
