our food

 FRESH FOOD, SOURCED LOCALLY     HEALTHY & AFFORDABLE FOOD SOLD YEAR ROUND     LOCALLY GROWN 

Through the strategic partnership with Saint Louis University’s Nutrition and Dietetics Department and HOSCO Food, the necessary resources are in place to begin operation.

Through our partnership with SLU, we have access to the expansive network of local farmers from whom SLU sources the produce, meat, and poultry for their Healthy Eating with Local Produce campaign. By combining our production and purchasing power with theirs, the St. Louis MetroMarket will be well stocked and able to negotiate low prices on healthy foods.

As an in-kind partner, SLU’s Nutrition and Dietetics Department will also serve an important role in making our produce shelf ready. We will utilize their 6,000 sq. ft. shared used instructional kitchen and their nutrition students to wash, bundle, and package much of the food that will be sold on the MetroMarket. Their students will work to develop value added products (turning tomatoes into marinara sauce) and healthy alternatives to TV dinners to be sold on the MetroMarket and be on hand to teach lessons on healthy eating through cooking demonstrations out in front of the MetroMarket, at the curbside.

HOSCO Food is an organic nutritional food company that specializes in sprouts and micro greens grown in aquaponic systems. For their part, HOSCO Food will supply the MetroMarket with microgreens, shrimp, and a variety of fish including Bluegill (a native fish), Trout, Perch, Redear, Large Mouth Bass, Tilapia and Pacu.
